About RaagAI

RaagAI is an innovative project that combines artificial intelligence with Indian classical music analysis. Our platform helps musicians, students, and enthusiasts understand the intricate patterns and structures of Indian classical music through advanced visualization and analysis tools.

Using state-of-the-art AI algorithms, we analyze various aspects of Indian classical music, including:

  • Raag identification and analysis
  • Rhythm pattern recognition
  • Instrumental technique analysis
  • Musical structure visualization
