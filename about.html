About RaagAI

RaagAI is a groundbreaking project that bridges the gap between traditional Indian classical music and modern technology. Our platform leverages artificial intelligence to analyze, recognize, and generate Indian classical music, making this rich cultural heritage more accessible to everyone.

What We've Built

In developing RaagAI, we've created a comprehensive platform that addresses several key aspects of Indian classical music:

  • Music Recognition: Our system can analyze Indian classical music in real-time, identifying the raga being played and providing detailed information about its characteristics.
  • Instrument Support: We've integrated support for multiple Indian classical instruments including Sitar, Tabla, Flute, Veena, Tanpura, Santoor, Kanjira, and Sarangi.
  • Music Generation: Users can generate music using various instruments, creating authentic Indian classical compositions.
  • Visual Analysis: Our platform provides visual representations of musical patterns, helping users understand the structure and flow of Indian classical music.

Technical Implementation

The RaagAI platform is built using cutting-edge web technologies:

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript for a responsive and intuitive user interface.
  • Audio Processing: Tone.js library for high-quality audio synthesis and manipulation.
  • AI Integration: Advanced algorithms for music recognition and analysis.
  • Visualization: Custom visualization tools to represent musical patterns and structures.
